Competition Participation Details

Registration Method:

The competition is open to youth and people of determination aged 16 to 40 (due to the month of October).

Competition Registration Fees:

For Egyptians: 100 Egyptian Pounds.

For non-Egyptians: 100 US Dollars (or 5,000 Egyptian Pounds), payable only if the contestants (foreigners) qualify for the final round.

¬ª¬ªThe competition is held in two stages¬´¬´

1- Preliminary Stage:

- The contestant in the preliminary round submits a video, taking into account the quality of the sound and image, presenting a song by Sayed Darwish, in addition to another song of their choice. Each song must be a maximum of one minute long.

- After completing the video preparation and editing, it is submitted via the competition registration page (next page). This is followed by creating a link to online file storage and sharing services (WeTransfer, Dropbox, Google Drive, OneDrive, SendAnywhere, etc.)
